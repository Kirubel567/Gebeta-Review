# Gebeta Review

Gebeta Review is a student-focused web platform designed to help students discover the best eats in and around their campus. From hidden cafeteria gems to top-rated street spots and student-run delivery startups, Gebeta Review allows users to explore and rate every bite their university has to offer.

## üöÄ Features

-   **Discover Food Spots**: Find on-campus meals, delivery options, and off-campus restaurants.
-   **Read Reviews**: Check out what other students are saying about local food businesses.
-   **Rate & Review**: Submit your own reviews to help the community make better food choices.
-   **Delivery Insights**: Compare delivery times, service quality, and fees.
-   **Responsive Design**: Optimized for both desktop and mobile devices.

## üìÇ Project Structure

The project follows a standard static website structure:

-   **`index.html`**: The main landing page featuring highlights and featured businesses.
-   **`reviews.html`**: Browse reviews for various food spots.
-   **`delivery.html`**: Information on delivery services available to students.
-   **`about.html`**: Information about the Gebeta Review project and team.
-   **`submitReviews.html`**: A form for users to submit their own reviews.
-   **`customerReview.html`**: Detailed view of customer reviews.
-   **`assets/`**: Contains static assets.
    -   **`css/`**: Modular CSS files organized by base, components, layout, and pages. `main.css` is the entry point.
    -   **`images/`**: Images used throughout the site.
-   **`components/`**: Reusable HTML components (e.g., navbar, footer) for consistent layout.
